# Images for Chapter 1 — Finding the Right Idea

Drop image files into `./assets/`. Suggested filenames in parentheses.

## Hero (`./assets/01-hero.png`)
**Used at:** Top of the chapter, full-width.
**Suggested ratio:** 16:9 or 3:1 (banner-style).
**Concept ideas:**
- A founder looking at a wall of sticky notes with problem statements (visual: messy ideation, real human, real notes — not a stock photo of a clean whiteboard).
- A handwritten list: "5 problems I've felt this week" with check marks and crossed-out items.
- A simple infographic: 4 boxes labeled "Felt it / Others have it / Will pay / Growing market" with a checkmark in each.

## Mid-article diagram (`./assets/01-tests.png`)
**Used at:** Inside the "four tests every idea needs to pass" section.
**Suggested ratio:** 4:3 or 1:1.
**Concept ideas:**
- A flowchart: Idea → Test 1 (felt it) → Test 2 (others have it) → Test 3 (willing to pay) → Test 4 (growing market) → "Build" or "Pick again."
- A simple grid scoring template (Frequency × Pain × WTP) with example numbers filled in for the air-mattress Airbnb scenario.

## Notes for whoever fills these in
- Keep imagery on-brand: dark background, accent purple `rgb(98, 20, 217)`, white/light text, generous whitespace.
- Avoid generic stock photos of "diverse team smiling at laptop." Real, specific imagery > stock.
- Shots.so or a Figma export at 2x retina works well for diagrams.
